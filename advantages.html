Advantages of Education in 2050

  1. Individual, self-paced curriculum enabling comfortable and effective learning.

  2. Learning environment that adheres to student 's needs.

  3. Technology that enriches learning potential and boost creativity.

  4. Frequent skill checks that will help to be in a constant study progress.

  5. E-learning will become much more affordable for people with limited budget.

  6. Distance learning will enable to mix study, work and family duties, and maintain the balance between them.

  7. Physical presence won't be required, so learning will become affordable from any corner of the world.

  8. Students will no longer be dependent upon a certain place to learn and will be able to study wherever they are.
