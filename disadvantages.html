DisAdvantages of Education in 2050

  1. There will be mechanical teachers in future, therefore the students will get the limited knowledge about the topics.

  2. Future schools will have the digital gadgets for studies that may cause harm to the eyes and students will be always seen stuck to the tablets and laptops.

  3. E-learning might cause social isolation. E-learning requires strong self-motivation and time management skills. There might be lack of communicational skill development among online students. Cheating prevention during online assessments might get complicated.

  4. Online learning may create a sense of isolation as everyone learns in their own manner. Some students possess the ability to work independently, while others find comfort with professors or their fellow students.

  5. Many teachers will be unemployed because robots will be teaching instead of humans.

  6. Students might end up taking wrong decisions as the internet can many times be misleading. So absence of human teachers might make a child emotionally unstable.

  7. Increased competition may create unnecessary stress among students surrounded by technology where the child might not be able to perform efficiently.
